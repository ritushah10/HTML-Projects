STEPS TO ACHIEVE THE PERFECT MAKEUP LOOK

PREP YOUR FACE
  • Wash face with cleanser and then dry face with a towel
  • Apply toner with a cotton pad
  • Dab any type of moisturizer and massage face
  • If you do get an allergic reaction from moisturizer,
    • Wash face with cold water
    • Apply ice to get rid of any swelling or bumps
  • Apply any type of primer



EYE MAKEUP
  1. Apply eyeshadow primer onto eyelids
  2. Fill in brows using a brow pomade and an angled brush
  3. Choose an eyeshadow palette
  4. Pick a color and use a blending brush to apply shadow onto lid
  5. Then apply eyeliner
  6. Finally, apply mascara or false lashes if desired



SKIN MAKEUP
  1. Using a large fluff brush, apply foundation (should be right skin tone)
  2. To get a better blend, use a beauty blender after using the brush
  3. Apply concealer to any blemishes, dark spots, or any pimples
  4. In order to contour
    • Use a contour pallete
    • Using the darkest shade, apply a line of the cream underneath cheekbones, jawline, top of forehead, and on sides of nose
    • Use a beauty blender to blend cream
  5. Use a loose setting powder to set foundation and contour
  6. Apply highlight on cheekbone, in the middle of forehead, tip of nose, and chin



SET SKIN
Rose Water
Spray rose water all over face after completed look
Suggested brands:Ulta Beauty, Mario Badescu
Setting Spray
Spray setting spray all over face after completed look
Suggested brands:Urban Decay, NYX, Too Faced
Tea Tree Oil
Make a mixture of tea tree oil and water and spray all over face after completed look
Suggested brands: The Body Shop, Lush, DIY
